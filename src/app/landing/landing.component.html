For Guest-Entry Application

Navigate to the Guest-Entry section to:

  • View all today onward guest entry applications with statuses and token numbers
  • Submit a new guest entry application

You can withdraw your application until the mess starting time for the respective meal.

Guest entries can be made for a maximum of 3 days in advance.

Access to the mess is available to a limited number guests, the number varies across hostels

Only individuals with an IITB card are permitted entry, and they must submit their ID cards at the security checkpoint. Individuals checking in are required to provide their complete details, and the notation of +1 or +2, along with any guest entry is not permitted.

Even if a token number is generated as proof, the submission of ID cards remains compulsory. Guests are required to write their names against their corresponding token numbers in the mess entry register kept at the security desk.
